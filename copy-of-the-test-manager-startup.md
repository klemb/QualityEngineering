::: {#comp-k986nyvd .Z_l5lU .ku3DBC .zQ9jDz .qvSjx3 .Vq6kJx .comp-k986nyvd .wixui-rich-text testid="richTextElement"}
[\"Low trust causes friction, whether it is caused by unethical behavior
or by ethical but incompetent behavior (because even good intentions can
never take the place of bad judgment). Low trust is the greatest cost in
life and in organizations. Low trust creates hidden agendas, politics,
interpersonal conflict, interdepartmental rivalries, win-lose thinking,
defensive and protective communication --- all of which reduce the speed
of trust. Low trust slows everything --- every decision, every
communication, and every relationship.\"]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Trust is a function of two things: character and competence. Character
includes your integrity, your motive, your intent with people.
Competence includes your capabilities, your skills, your results, your
track record.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[The competency framework is there to help you incresing your
capabilities and skill as a Test Engineer, helping you to build trusting
relationships within the organisation.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Capabilities (Are you relevant?) - Capable people are credible.
Capability is more than skill; it includes:]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

-   [Talents - What are my unique, native
    strengths?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

-   [Attitutes - Do I approach work with energy and the determination to
    contribute?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

-   [Skills - Do I continuously upgrade my
    skills?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

-   [Knowledge - What am I doing to stay current and keep
    learning?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

-   [Style - Does my style of doing things get in the way or ease the
    path?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}
:::

## [[[Building Your Capabilities]{.wixui-rich-text__text style="color:#292929;"}]{.wixui-rich-text__text style="font-family:avenir-lt-w01_35-light1475496,sans-serif;"}]{.wixui-rich-text__text style="font-size:44px;"} {#building-your-capabilities .font_2 .wixui-rich-text__text style="font-size:44px;"}
:::

::: {#comp-k986nyvl .Z_l5lU .ku3DBC .zQ9jDz .qvSjx3 .Vq6kJx .comp-k986nyvl .wixui-rich-text testid="richTextElement"}
1.  [Run with your strengths.\
    Feed your strengths. What are they? Where are your natural talents?
    What can you do best? Starve your weakness by teaming with others
    who are strong where you\'re weak.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

2.  [Keep yourself relevant.\
    Match your stregths to opportunities. Where can you make a unique,
    high-value contribution?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

3.  [Know where you\'re going.\
    Specify that different-in-kind contribution and keep the vision of
    it before you from now on.​]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

## [[[Improving your Results]{.wixui-rich-text__text style="color:#292929;"}]{.wixui-rich-text__text style="font-family:avenir-lt-w01_35-light1475496,sans-serif;"}]{.wixui-rich-text__text style="font-size:44px;"} {#improving-your-results .font_2 .wixui-rich-text__text style="font-size:44px;"}
:::

::: {#comp-k986nyvt .Z_l5lU .ku3DBC .zQ9jDz .qvSjx3 .Vq6kJx .comp-k986nyvt .wixui-rich-text testid="richTextElement"}
[What\'s your track record?  Clearly, your credibility rests on teh
results you get in your work. But because you can do everything right
and still fail, make sure you define results
accurately.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

1.  [Take responsibility for results\
    Adopt a \"results mind-set\" rather than an \"activity mind-set\".
    Ask yourself: WIll what I\'m doing now lead to teh results I want or
    am I just staying busy?]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

2.  [Expect to win\
    Openly express confidence in yourself and others. Clearly define
    what winning consists of. Create an emotional climate of high
    expectations.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

3.  [Finish strong\
    Drop out of the \"culture of quitting\" and the \"victim
    mentality\". Stay strong at the end when everything\'s on the
    line.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
    style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[ARCIFE (Accountable, Responsible, Consulted, Informed, Facilitator,
Expert) analysis is a refinement on the standard RACI (Responsible,
Accountable, Consulted, Informed) analysis for engagement in an
activity. The ARCIFE terms are defined below:]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[[​]{.wixGuard .wixui-rich-text__text}]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[A -- Accountable]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Leadership, making sure the activity is done, and done right. Often the
accountable person delegates the actual doing. There should only be one
accountable person for a specific activity /
issue.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[R -- Responsible]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[People (maybe different roles) that do the activity (so if the output
of the activity is a report, these are the authors). Responsibility can
be shared in a team.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[C -- Consulted]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Engaged in the work; may provide input, or (more likely) either apply
specific technical / domain knowledge to assist with the activity, or
they use / act on the outcome of the process and influence
it]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[I -- Informed]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Needs to know either that the work product is produced, or the outcome
(or part of outcome and decision from process
activity.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[[​]{.wixGuard .wixui-rich-text__text}]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[The next two terms are in a different dimension to the level of
descending importance above. To embed Test Engineering, individuals may
have to help the team apply specific Test Engineering techniques, or
define and develop best practice.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[[​]{.wixGuard .wixui-rich-text__text}]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[F -- Facilitator / Coach]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Lead workshops or discussions applying Test Engineering Approach (with
people from other skills) and build consolidated and agreed models. This
aspect includes sufficient expertise and knowledge in Test Engineering
Approach (process and techniques) to select the most appropriate for the
situation, considering both the nature of the system of interest (under
test), and for the "testing" competence and experience of the team they
are working with.]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[E -- Expert]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}

[Develops / explains or teaches methods and process in this area, and
advances the state of the art. Considered a specialist in the
competency]{.wixui-rich-text__text
style="font-family:helvetica-w01-roman,helvetica-w02-roman,helvetica-lt-w10-roman,sans-serif;"}
